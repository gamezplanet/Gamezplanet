Data Deletion Instructions

GamezPlanet respects your privacy and complies with Meta Platform Policies. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data collected through our Facebook integration.

What Data We Collect

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Email address (if permitted)
  • Game progress and achievements
  • Basic interaction and usage data

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, you have two options:

Option 1: Through Facebook
Go to your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → Find GamezPlanet → Click "Remove" to revoke access and request data deletion.

Option 2: Email Us Directly
Send an email with the subject "Data Deletion Request" to:

admin@gamezplanet.xyz

Include your Facebook User ID or the email address you used to log in.

What Happens Next

  • Requests are processed within 7 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your data is deleted
  • All your personal information will be permanently removed from our servers
  • Your game progress and achievements will be lost

Questions? Contact us at info@gamezplanet.xyz for assistance with data deletion or privacy concerns.
